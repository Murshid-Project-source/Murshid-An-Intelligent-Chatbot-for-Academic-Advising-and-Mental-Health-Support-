# Murshid-An-Intelligent-Chatbot-for-Academic-Advising-and-Mental-Health-Support-
Many students face academic difficulties and psychological stress throughout their education. For example, students may lack knowledge of university regulations or a safe space to express their feelings or seek guidance when facing academic problems. To address this issue, the project proposes developing an intelligent application.
